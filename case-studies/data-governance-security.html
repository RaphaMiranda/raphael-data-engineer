The Challenge

A rapidly scaling fintech enterprise faced a critical crossroads: their data access patterns were ungoverned, with manual SQL permissions and zero visibility into PII (Personally Identifiable Information) exposure.

The goal was to move from "reactive security" to "proactive governance"—enabling automated auditing while maintaining the agility of 12 internal data teams.
