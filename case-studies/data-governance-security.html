The Challenge

A rapidly scaling organisation (aged care & fintech) faced a critical crossroads: data access was ungoverned, with manual SQL permissions and zero visibility into PII (Personally Identifiable Information) exposure across their Azure data estate.

The engineering goal was to move from "reactive security" to "proactive governance" — automating access enforcement and audit trails while maintaining agility for multiple internal data teams. Additionally, ISO 27001 certification required formal ISMS policies, evidence of security controls, and cross-functional committee review.
