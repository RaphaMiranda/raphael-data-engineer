The "Slow Dashboard" Crisis

Business users were abandoning reports due to 30-second refresh times and inconsistent metric definitions across departments. The underlying Redshift + Athena models were structurally unfit for Power BI's in-memory VertiPaq engine.

warning Legacy Architecture Flaws

  • > Snowflake schemas with 40+ joins per visual
  • > Row-by-row iterative DAX measures (FILTER/SUMX)
  • > Excessive calculated columns inflating in-memory footprint
  • > No certified dataset tier — every team had their own KPI definitions

Engineering the Solution

hub

Data Denormalisation

Collapsed complex relational schemas in Redshift into high-performance star schemas. Reduced the VertiPaq engine cardinality burden by 60% and eliminated all multi-join fanout queries.

speed

DAX Tuning

Replaced heavy row-context DAX (FILTER / SUMX) with set-based logic and Variables (VAR) to eliminate redundant sub-calculations. All measures reviewed with Tabular Editor and DAX Studio.

verified

Certified Dataset Tier

Established a "Certified Dataset" governance framework ensuring one central source of truth for critical KPIs like "Adjusted Net Revenue" — eliminating metric fragmentation across 12 departments.
