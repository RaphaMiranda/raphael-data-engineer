The "Slow Dashboard" Crisis

The organization was facing a "Data Trust Deficit." Business users were abandoning reports due to 30-second refresh times and inconsistent metric definitions across different departments.

warning Legacy Architecture Flaws

  • > Snowflake schemas with 40+ joins per visual
  • > Row-by-Row iterative DAX measures (FILTER/SUMX)
  • > Excessive calculated columns inflating memory

Engineering the Solution

hub

Data Denormalization

Collapsed complex relational structures in Synapse into high-performance analytical star schemas, reducing the query engine burden by 60%.

speed

DAX Tuning

Replaced heavy, row-context DAX with set-based logic and utilized Variables (VAR) to prevent redundant calculation sub-steps.

verified

Governance Framework

Implemented a 'Certified Dataset' tier, ensuring one central source of truth for critical KPIs like 'Adjusted Net Revenue'.
